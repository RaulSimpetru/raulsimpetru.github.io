About Me

I am a biomedical engineer with extensive competence in machine learning and software development. Most of my software skils are self tought through years of practicing, while my hardware skils come for more classical training in university. My strongest skills are my ability to quickly prototype deep learning problems and put them into practice. These skills are rounded off by my exceptional code workflow, which helps turn the prototype into a product.

I am currently working at the N-squared Lab, FAU Erlangen-Nürnberg to create the best kinematics and kinetics prediction model using only the electrical activity of human muscles.

  • PyThorch-Lighting
  • MLflow

  • High Performance Computing (HPC) Clusters
  • NVIDIA Jetson
